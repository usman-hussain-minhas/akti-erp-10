# Phase 6A-6C Stage 1 Extension Catalog v1

Source authority: `docs/process/phases/cross_phase/esbla_phase_6a_6c_consolidated_amendment_v1.md`

- 6A-X1: Person Graph, multi-participant. One profile per participant type per Person; multiple tenant organizations per Person; collective accounts with representative; scoped restrictions.
- 6A-X2: Tiered verification. T0-T3 plus guardian-linked verification states; provider-neutral KYC adapter registry; appeals and de-dup review path.
- 6A-X3: Evidence ledger hardening. Context-at-write fields; tombstone/redaction/legal-hold operations; counterparty notice events; delete/redaction/erasure/hold distinctions.
- 6A-X4: Reputation interpretation service. Three-layer reputation separation; versioned interpretation; decay/confidence/exposure-floor parameters; disclosure profiles and adverse-action hooks.
- 6A-X5: Communication Gateway. Cross-tenant audited channel types; guardian-mode restrictions; opt-out enforced at gateway for every channel.
- 6A-X6: Configuration constraints. Canonical defaults, declared overrides, save-time combination validation, and re-baselining offer mechanism.
- 6A-X7: AI proxy dual-plane. Three tenant AI states; chat/action credit metering; BYO-API consent; Gatekeeper interception for consequence-bearing actions.
- 6A-X8: Foundry cross-tenant activation. Cross-tenant capability grants; tenant-activation-aware frontend chunk registration; two-phase uninstall unchanged.
- 6B-X1: Marketplace transaction infrastructure. Escrow Saga primitives; platform-fee line item; multi-currency support; high-value flag in billing objects.
- 6B-X2: Payout rails. Provider-neutral payout adapters; per-market capability matrix; payout failures recorded without reputation consequence.
- 6B-X3: Cross-tenant invoicing. Tenant-to-tenant and tenant-to-freelancer invoicing with evidence linkage and invoice immutability.
- 6B-X4: Billing honesty surfaces. AI cost surfacing in tenant billing UI and bond ledger objects as segregated refundable balances.
- 6B-X5: Pricing presentations. Multiple pricing presentations; consumption honesty; FX gain/loss remains Phase 6B scope.
- 6C-X1: Workspace-level working copy. Workspace ownership types; role-scoped access; transfer as permission change with snapshots and milestone manifests.
- 6C-X2: Structured agreements. Objective/subjective criteria flags; criteria library; zero-objective warning; IP terms and agreement-clarity markers.
- 6C-X3: AI verification hooks. Shadow-advisory accounting; confidence gate; exclusions; auto-acceptance notice ladder; scope-narrowing triggers.
- 6C-X4: Employment to reputation linkage. Employment records emit evidence to three-layer reputation; anti-retaliation meta-pattern instrumentation; crossing disabled by default.
- 6C-X5: Dispute and appeals scaffolding. Appeal ladder hooks; pre-panel irreversibility bar; power-imbalance patterns as flags to review, never auto-consequence.
- 6C-X6: Cross-tenant scheduling and recruitment. Gateway-mediated cross-tenant availability and recruitment through disclosure profiles only.
