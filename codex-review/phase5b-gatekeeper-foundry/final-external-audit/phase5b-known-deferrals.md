# Phase 5B Known Deferrals

The following items are intentionally deferred and are not hidden blockers for Phase 5B closure:

- Phase 5C frontend excellence and pixel-level UI work.
- Phase 6A Golden Module implementation.
- Phase 6B+ business modules, including Admissions, HR, Finance, or other domain workflows.
- Marketplace/public module store behavior.
- Production deployment and operational rollout.
- Real external adapters and production provider activation.
- Production WhatsApp activation.
- Runtime AI provider calls beyond the governed proxy/stub boundary.
- Production secrets or credentials.

The previously accepted Tier 2 event-envelope gap is no longer open at closure. It was documented at `P5B-T2-GATE` and closed by the Tier 3 Gatekeeper and Foundry retrofit work before `P5B-T3-GATE`.
